# 🎨 CodeAlpha Graphic Design Internship

This repository contains the graphic design tasks I completed during my Graphic Design Internship at CodeAlpha. These projects helped me strengthen my creativity, design thinking, branding knowledge, and typography skills while working with industry-standard design tools.

---

## 📌 Task 1: Logo Redesign

### Objective
Redesign an existing logo while preserving its brand identity and giving it a modern, visually appealing look.

### What I Learned
- Applied modern logo design principles.
- Maintained brand consistency while enhancing visual appeal.
- Explored different logo variations and design concepts.
- Improved my understanding of branding and visual identity.
- Enhanced creativity and attention to detail.

### Tools Used
- Adobe Illustrator
- CorelDRAW
- Figma
- Canva

## ✍️ Task 2: Typography Practice

### Objective
